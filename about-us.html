Direct Billing

What is Direct Billing?

Direct billing allows us to submit your claim (the cost of your session) directly to your insurance provider, saving you the hassle of paying upfront and submitting claims yourself. This allows you to focus on your treatment without having to worry about covering the full session cost in advance or reimbursement delays. It’s a convenient option if your insurance plan covers counselling/social work services, but it’s important to check with both your insurer to ensure eligibility and understand any coverage limits or co-pays.
