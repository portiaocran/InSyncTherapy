About Us

We are not who we are by chance, but rather by our experiences and how our experiences shape us.

Here at InSync Therapy and Wellness we place a major emphasis on providing support through a culturally responsive trauma-informed lens to help you gain a deeper awareness and understanding of yourself and your relationships.




Our
Values

Empathy

Prioritizing empathy and care in every interaction

Inclusion

Providing a welcoming and accepting space for everyone of all backgrounds and identities

Transparency

Clear, honest and open communication

Person-Centred

Working together to create individualized plans that are tailored to meet the unique needs and goals of each person, couple and family

Empowerment through psychoeducation

Providing knowledge and resources to help you better understand and care for your mental health
