About Us

We are not who we are by chance, but rather by our experiences and how our experiences have shaped us.
Here at InSync Therapy and Wellness we place a major emphasis on providing support through a culturally responsive trauma-informed lens to help you gain a deeper awareness and understanding of yourself.
Self-awareness is grounding, and knowledge is powerful. By exploring our past experiences and understanding how they have shaped our automatic thoughts, belief systems, behaviors, self-esteem, attachment styles in relationships, and other crucial aspects of our identity, we can gain deeper insight into ourselves and how we navigate our lives. This self-awareness lays the foundation for transformational change.
We can get to the root of our problems helping us unlearn habits that no longer serve us and learn new habits, tools, and resources that better align with our current lives. This process can lead to healthier, more meaningful outcomes, enhancing our mental health and wellness in the present and future.
