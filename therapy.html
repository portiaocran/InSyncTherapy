Individual Therapy

50 minutes • $160

At InSync Therapy and Wellness we understand that individual therapy is a deeply personal and transformative journey. One of our main intentions at InSync Therapy and Wellness is to help you strengthen your connection to your authentic self and live life with greater clarity, purpose and fulfillment.

Couples Therapy

60 minutes • $200

At InSync Therapy and Wellness we offer a space for you and your partner to strengthen your relationship by healing past wounds, develop healthy communication styles, and navigate the complexities of your shared lives together. We guide couples in recognizing and unlearning harmful patterns, resolve lingering conflicts and increase vulnerability, resulting in a deeper emotional connection and greater understanding of each other’s needs.

  • Parenting Challenges

Family Therapy
