About Me

Welcome, it’s nice to meet you.

Priscilla Ocranshe/her/hers M.S.W, R.S.W

My name is Priscilla Ocran and I am a Registered Social Worker, therapist and Founder of InSync Therapy and Wellness.

I received my Master of Social Work specializing in Mental Health and Health from the University of Toronto. I have been a part of research teams that studied the over representation of Black children and youth in the child welfare system and examining the impacts of the MeToo movement regarding assault disclosures in Canada.

With over 10 years of experience in helping individuals and families, I provide compassionate, evidence-based care to a wide range of diverse communities. I place an emphasis on supporting BIPOC communities, particularly BIPOC women who often face greater barriers and receive less help due to their intersecting identities.

I work from a person-centered trauma-informed lens, tailoring the care I provide to meet the unique needs of every individual, couple and family that comes into this space. I use a combination of approaches that that integrate working with the body the mind and relationships to help facilitate regulation and healing.

I strive to create an environment where you can come as you are with no judgement and full acceptance. You are more than enough and your experiences, the hard ones, the sad ones, the happy ones and the messy ones all deserve a space to be heard and validated because your authentic self matters.
